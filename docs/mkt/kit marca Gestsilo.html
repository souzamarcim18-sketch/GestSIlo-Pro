Purpose & context Marcio is an agronomist by training who built GestSilo — an agricultural management SaaS platform for rural producers in Brazil — with Claude's assistance, and is actively learning software development and business through the process. GestSilo covers silage management, herd/livestock tracking, pasture rotation, crop management, inputs, financials, fleet, forage balance, calculators, and agronomic consulting features, with a role-based access system (Admin, Viewer, Operator). The target market is small and medium rural producers in Brazil. Marcio is both the product owner and sole developer. Key business context: GestSilo is essentially feature-complete and in final pre-launch preparation Stripe is being migrated from test to production mode; business registration (MEI/ME under Simples Nacional) is a pending prerequisite Marcio operates as an individual (pessoa física) and is evaluating MEI registration, with awareness that MEI's permitted activities may not cover software development — ME under Simples Nacional noted as the more legally sound path at scale A contact inside CCPR (a large agricultural cooperative in Minas Gerais) represents a strategic partnership opportunity; the recommended approach is to validate with paying users first before approaching CCPR formally Domain fluency: Marcio uses Portuguese agritech and Brazilian business terminology naturally (MEI, PJ, MRR, Simples Nacional, fazenda, silagem, rebanho, lavoura, cocho, balanço forrageiro, etc.) Current state Final pre-launch tasks remaining: MEI or ME business registration on gov.br Business bank account setup Stripe production migration: create production products/prices for the four plans (Start, Campo, Pro, Max), register webhook at https://gestsilo.com/api/stripe/webhook, configure Customer Portal, update all Vercel environment variables (STRIPESECRETKEY, NEXTPUBLICSTRIPEPUBLISHABLEKEY, STRIPEWEBHOOKSECRET, and all STRIPEPRICEID*) Google Search Console setup and SEO metadata configuration (identified but not yet started) Post-onboarding empty states (identified but not yet started) Subscription plan structure (finalized): Start (free), Campo (R$49/month), Pro (R$89/month), Max (R$119/month — includes one online meeting every 2 months with the GestSilo team) 17% annual discount across all paid plans (≈ 2 months free) Plan is tied to the fazenda, not the individual user; Instruções

O que é o GestSilo GestSilo é um SaaS de gestão agrícola completa para pequenos e médios produtores brasileiros, com foco especializado em gestão de silos de silagem e balanço forrageiro. Resolve o problema de perdas na silagem por falta de controle do ponto de colheita, qualidade fermentativa e fornecimento ao rebanho, além de oferecer visão integrada da propriedade rural (talhões, frota, financeiro, rebanho, insumos, produtos e planejamento). O produto é acessado via web e PWA (offline-ready), por produtores com baixa familiaridade tecnológica, muitas vezes em campo, com sol forte e luvas. Fase atual Plataforma funcional com múltiplos módulos em produção (Vercel). Prioridade: evolução contínua dos módulos core com qualidade, sem over-engineering. Decisões devem favorecer velocidade de entrega sem comprometer a base técnica. Base de testes com 704+ casos passando (Vitest). Público-alvo Produtor rural brasileiro (pecuária leiteira e de corte), pequeno a médio porte, com acesso via smartphone. Baixa familiaridade com tecnologia. Vocabulário simples, direto, sem jargão técnico na interface.
